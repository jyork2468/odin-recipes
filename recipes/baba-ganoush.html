Baba Ganoush

Description

Baba ganoush, also known as baba ganouj, muttabal, or salat hatzilim, is a creamy eggplant dip made by mixing tender roasted eggplant and nutty tahini with garlic, citrus, and spices. Grilling the eggplant over an open flame will give more flabor, and allowing the dip to chill in the fridge will cause it to thicken and the flavors will meld together.

This recipe has a prep time of 15 minutes, a cook time of 20 minutes, and a drainning and resting time of 1 hour. The approximate total time for this recipe is 1 hour and 35 minutes. The recipe will easily serve 6 or more people.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 1/4 cup tahini paste
  • 1 lemon, squeezed and juiced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 Tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1 tsp sumac
  • 3/4 tsp Aleppo pepper or red pepper flakes
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • toasted pine nuts, for garnish
